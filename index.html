Dancing in the Midnight

Dancing in the Midnight is an interactive design project that envisions a future where renewable energy plays a central role in everyday life. Our goal was to harness kinetic energy from human movement to power all the electricity needed at a playground. We designed a miniature dance floor that reacts to the presence of people, with dynamic lighting and audio that change as users step on the tiles, creating an engaging, collaborative experience that becomes more vibrant with each participant.

Moviewer

Moviewer is a web application designed to simplify how users discover, organize, and track movies or TV shows across multiple streaming platforms. The app aggregates information using an existing API, presenting details like availability, IMDb ratings, and streaming locations in a clear, user-friendly format. By allowing users to create personalized playlists, Moviewer offers a streamlined way to manage "watch later" lists, much like Goodreads for movies.

Cleo

Cleo is an interactive system designed to serve as a personalized cooking mentor, guiding users through the learning-to-cook process and enhancing their overall cooking experience. The system offers real-time, hands-on assistance, making cooking more intuitive and enjoyable, especially for beginners.
