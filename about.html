Portfolio About Me
Izabelle Lundqvist

Hi, I’m an Engineer and UX/Product Designer with a multidisciplinary background in Interactive Media Technology from KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My passion lies at the intersection of technology, design, and sustainability—creating innovative, user-centered experiences that not only meet user needs but also drive sustainable innovation and make a meaningful impact. Whether it’s designing human-powered prototypes or digital interactive products, I thrive on turning complex challenges into creative solutions.

Sports have always been a big part of my life—from playing NCAA Division 1 football in the U.S. to working on live sports broadcasts for SHL and Allsvenskan. Studying abroad in Spain and the U.S. has shaped my collaborative, open-minded approach, and I’m always excited to work with diverse teams to bring ideas to life.
